Mokhtari Station

Installation of station

PreviousNext

Tehran Bus Rapid Transit was officially inaugurated in 2008 in order to improve Tehran's public transportation. 
Line 7 BRT starts from the railway station in the south and ends in Tajrish Square in the north of Tehran. This line is one of the longest and busiest BRT lines. This line was not initially suitable for BRT and therefore requires basic adaptation measures.
Mokhtari station, one of the active stations of this line, has been installed by the Borj & Pay Semin company.

  • Date: January 2016
  • Client: Tehran Municipality of Region 11
  • Category: public transportation
